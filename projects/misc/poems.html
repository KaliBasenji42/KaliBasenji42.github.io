Poems

The Machine

One machine of flesh
Reducible into smaller infinitely
Complex machines
Howling out soul
Extracting out soul
Stripping them
To a new world and
Reverting them to
An old world

Voyager

Rolling over an artificial surface
Hugged by mist
Hugged by darkness
A neighbor quickly passing, just as complex

No other light
For ages
"Where are you going?"
I do not know

Too much to see

Collision of water
Vocalization of avians and mammalians
Wind brushing flora

Wind brushing skin
Course bark
Squish of moss

Low pressure atmosphere

Filled with mist
Carrying soil
