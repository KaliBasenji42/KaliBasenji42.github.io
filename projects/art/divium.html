Divum is a chickadee. His name roughly translates to "sky" or "open air" in Latin. As of now Divum does not have much background, this is one of my first drawings and I just wanted to try my hand at an anthro bird. I started with a normal chickadee (Bird Side) and when I had the colors and patterns down, drew the anthro form while checking to make sure the proportions looked right.
